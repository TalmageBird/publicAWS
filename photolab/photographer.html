About the Photographer

Early Life:

Clark Kent grew up in Kansas and was bullied during his early years. Then he saved a school bus full of kids and he made one friend. During his high school years clark enjoyed farming, fixing farm equiment, cleaning the farm house, running around the farm, eating farm food, selling stuff at the farmers market, collecting farm fresh eggs, and just hanging out on the farm.

Present:

Today Clark Kent is a photographer and writer for the Daily Planet. He used to have a girlfriend but she dumped him and now he is single. He lives in New York with his two cats, Zodd and Kryptonite. He enjoys collecting antique phonebooths, visiting North Korea, and ping pong. His parents are unknown, and for that matter, any family relations of his are unknown. His dislikes are homework projects that take way to long and yogurt.
